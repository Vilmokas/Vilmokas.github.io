SketchyMaze

First game I made with Laeti Team.

On June 25th, 2019 I join an indie game dev team. This team consists of 2 programmers, 1 artist and 1 game desginer. We just love making making games, so we desided to create a team and make games together in our spare time. After some time, we desided on our first project. And here it is.

About the game

This game is fairly simple. Player has to complete levels by finding his way through a dungeon. While doing so, he has to collect ligthers, so his light doesnt go out.

Player will also encounter some monsters, that are trying to kill him. As player has no means to fight, he will have to dodge them or use his enviroment to kill the enemies.

Game has multiple levels that are being updated. You can download our game on Google Play
