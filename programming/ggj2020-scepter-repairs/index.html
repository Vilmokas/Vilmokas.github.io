Global Game Jam 2020 - Scepter Repairs

August 18, 2022
Demo 

Description#

Global Game Jam 2020 game made focusing on the theme “Repair”. In this game player has to repair other mages’ scepters by saying the correct combination of spells. If player doesn’t repair enough scepters, dungeon boss gets mads and starts attacking, ending the game.

Features#

  • 3d point and click movement
  • Spell casting by selecting runes shows on screen
