Global Game Jam 2020

Created a game in GGJ2020 with another programmer from Laeti Team

This year I participated in Global Game Jam 2020 again, tho with another team and in different location. I went to Kaunas for this event with another programmer from Laeti Team.

About the game

Game was created in Unity. Most of the models were taken from another project of ours, because we had no artists on the team this year. Game Jam theme was "Repair", so after some brainstorming we decided to make a wizard support desk game, where player has to fix other wizard's scepter with the right combination of runes.

When you select the right two runes that are needed in correct order, you will gain 10 mana. If you fail to sellect or take too long, you will lose 5 mana. At 0 mana, your boss, the demon, will get angry and fire you from your job. By fire, I mean, literally FIRE you. He will cast a fire spell on your and burn you alive.

This year was the first year when I actually finished a game in 48 hours. Previous jams weren't so succesful, so I'm really happy about this one. If you would like to know more about the game I and my team mate made in this year's game jam or even play it, check out our GGJ 2020 Page.
